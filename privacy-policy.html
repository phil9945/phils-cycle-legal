PhilsCycleApp Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

PhilsCycleApp ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

PhilsCycleApp is designed to operate primarily on your device. We do not collect or store personal data on external servers.

Location Data

The app uses your device’s location to:

  • track rides
  • calculate distance and speed
  • display your route on a map

Location data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

User-Provided Information

You may optionally provide:

  • ride notes
  • emergency contact phone number

This information is stored only on your device.

2. Data Storage

All ride data, including:

  • routes
  • ride statistics
  • notes

is stored locally on your device.

We do not operate any servers that collect or store your ride data.

3. Data Sharing

PhilsCycleApp does not share your personal data.

You may choose to share your data manually by:

  • exporting PDF ride summaries
  • exporting CSV files
  • sending your location via SMS

These actions are initiated by you.

4. Subscriptions

The app may offer optional premium features via in-app subscription.

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses:

  • Apple Maps (for map display)
  • Apple’s in-app purchase system (for subscriptions)

These services may collect data according to Apple’s policies.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We rely on Apple’s iOS security framework to protect your data.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • disable location access in device settings
  • delete your ride data within the app
  • uninstall the app to remove locally stored data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new effective date.
